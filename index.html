Man Moves Entire Armadillo With His Mind!

Nov 7th, 2017

Masses gathered, last Wednesday, in Dillon, Montana, to watch a talented charlatan move a lime armadillo with his mind. This thrill-fest accumulated 100,000 spectators to join what has become known as the woodstock for telekinetics. Georgy Dorshy, age 31, stared down a 10 pound Armadillo for 6 1/2 hours, when suddenly, the magic began! Once specatator claimed, "This event caused me to quit my job and re-evaluate my life. I've been staring at spoons for 6 days, since this festival. It was wild, man!".

Around 9:47 GMT, the brightly colored armidillo moved approximately 8 inches across the grassy plain. The crowd erupted into an absolute frenzy that echoed the likes of the Russian Revolution. Cold ones were immediately cracked and an all-night celebration party followed. "I'm just a really good looking guy who can move tangible objects with my mind. Thats it!", Georgy said. "I'm pretty sure that armadillo is well aware of my greatness.", Georgy quickly added. He later delivered a heart-felt message to his beloved audience, "I like money!".

Why Ryan's Computer is the Worst!

Nov 7th, 2017

Was it fished out of a lake? Perhaps salvaged at the local disposal facility? Although the facts are still unclear, what is certain is that Ryan's computer is absolutely horrible! You may inquire, "But why? Isn't it a brand new Dell, Ryan?". Well, yes! Yes it is! 2 months old, to be exact. It is this baffling detail that has scientist, world-wide, scrambling for their labcoats, to investigate the shameful reason of why Ryan's computer is a joke.

Are you ready? Can you handle the truth that has been perpetuating this occult-ish secret for ages? Here it goes: Ryan's computer is garbage because the mouse spontaneously decide to jump around the document as Ryan is typing. This is an incredibly annoying feature. Almost as annoying people who refer to themselves in the third person, like I did in the previous sentence. Even as I type this article, the keyboard is throwing a coupe and regaining control of its faculties. THIS. IS. A. TRAGEDY!
